About Kru Welding

My name is Ryan Krussow, owner of Kru Welding LLC. I've been welding for 6+ years and I love it! I started out at a Technical College to learn the basics of welding, and ended up finishing the program at a young age to pursue a career. After working for multiple welding companies, I decided to break off and start my own business where I could apply all of my crafting skills, including creativity and resourcefulness. This is perfect for custom fabrication and repair. I am dedicated to high quality and customer satisfaction to build my business and support my growing family.
